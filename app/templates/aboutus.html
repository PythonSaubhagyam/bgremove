ABOUT US

Who We Are

Photoliz is a photo editing software that removes unwanted image background and automatically proposes a randomly astonished image background that lets anyone in awe. The software is powered by the artificial intelligence that lets you edit your photo in few seconds.

Photoliz is a unprecedently seen photo editor software that is built with social media sharing which allows users to share immediately their images to friends and family. Aside for the social sharing feature, you also get an autoresponder just inside the user’s interface which allows you to directly email your images to your clients without leaving your software. And many more amazing features to ease your editing tasks.

Photoliz is a continual growth and development software that aims to become one of the leading photo editing software. Photoliz focuses on the growing needs and demands of users because they are the primary stakeholders.

Mission Statement

Develop a user-friendly software that enables anyone edit their pictures more efficiently.

Goals

Photoliz primary goal consists of offering software that help users save time and money while keeping high quality service rendered.

The secondary goal is to keep updated our services by adding new features on the existing software; and at the same time creating new software on demand of our customers.

Values

Innovation: we use the ingenuity of people and the Artificial Intelligence to keep bring new ideas that respond to the need of the market. At Photoliz, we welcome ideas of our peoples and stakeholders. We analyze each and see how we can improve what we are doing.

Collaboration: the success of Photoliz is coming from our team working to make it possible. We believe that together we are stronger and for that reason, we count on every individual in the team.

Empowering: at Photoliz, we consider each team member as an entrepreneur who takes care his tasks and customers under his portfolio.
